Simple Omelette Recipe

An omelette (also spelled omelet) is a dish made

from eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan.

Preparation Time

  • Total : Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 3 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter and oil
  • optional fillings:Cheese Dice

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs in bowl
  2. Heat the pan
  3. cook the omelette
